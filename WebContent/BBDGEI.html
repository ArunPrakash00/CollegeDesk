BBDGEI Group

The cause of Education, based on the ideals of "Babuji" is being nourished by various Educational Institutions, under the umbrella of Babu Banarasi Das Group of Educational Institutions (BBDGEI), that have come together to pool in their resources and intellectual capital, creating a Campus full of synergy and meaningful learning. The Institutions take full advantage of collective efforts and optimization of resources while still maintaining their distinct identities in a spirit of camaraderie and healthy competition.
  • Babu Banarasi Das National Institute of Technology and Management, Lucknow
  • Babu Banarasi Das Northern India Institute of Technology, Lucknow
  • (Formerly Northern India Engineering College, Lucknow)
  • Babu Banarasi Das Educational Society's Group of Institutions, Lucknow
  • Northern India Engineering College, New Delhi
OUR INITIATIVES FOR PROVIDING STRONG SUPPORT TO ACTIVITIES PROMOTING ACADEMICS HAS RESULTED IN
  • Strong Faculty resource of more than 1500 qualified and highly experienced Faculty supported by double the number of Technical & Teaching Support Staff.
  • State-of-the-art Computing Facilities with 5000 PCs, Well-equipped Modern Departmental Laboratories, 24x7 40Mbps Internet Bandwidth Connectivity through multiple ISPs, Wi-fi Campus & 5 MW Captive Power Generation Capacity.
  • Rich & Well Stocked Central & Departmental Libraries with more than 2,00,000 Books, Periodicals and Journals in print as well as in electronic form with Online subscription of IEEE, INDEST, Science Direct, Bentham etc.
  • Conduction of regular Seminars & Workshops, Entrepreneurship Development Programms for Students, Faculty Development Programs, Management Development Programs (MDP) & Consultancy Services to Industry
  • An excellent Research Environment with a number of Research Projects Sponsored & Funded by various National & International Agencies.
